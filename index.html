Monster Survivors: Survive the Horde

An action-packed survival puzzle game where you battle endless waves of monsters using strategy and skill.

Action Puzzle Strategy Survival Monsters

How to Play

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character. Avoid monsters while collecting power-ups and experience points to level up your abilities. Survive as long as possible against increasingly difficult waves of enemies.

Game Features

  • Multiple character classes with unique abilities
  • Procedurally generated levels
  • Dozens of upgrades and power-ups
  • Challenging boss battles
  • Increasing difficulty as you progress

Tips & Tricks

  • Focus on movement speed upgrades early
  • Don't get cornered by monster hordes
  • Prioritize area damage abilities for crowd control
  • Health regeneration is crucial for long runs
  • Learn enemy patterns to anticipate their movements

About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an addictive roguelike puzzle game that challenges players to survive against endless waves of monsters. As you progress, you'll unlock new characters, abilities, and face increasingly difficult challenges.

The game combines elements of action, strategy, and puzzle-solving as you navigate through procedurally generated environments. Each playthrough offers a unique experience with different enemy combinations and power-up opportunities.

With its simple controls but deep gameplay mechanics, Monster Survivors is easy to learn but difficult to master. How long can you survive the monster horde?
